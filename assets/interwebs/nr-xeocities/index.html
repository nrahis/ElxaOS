Latest Experiment: Making Bismuth Crystals

After melting down some Pepto-Bismol tablets and extracting the bismuth, I was able to create these beautiful iridescent crystals through careful temperature control and cooling. The geometric patterns are a natural result of the crystal structure!

C₇H₅BiO₄ + 2NaOH → BiONO₃ + 2H₂O
2BiONO₃ + 3H₂ → 2Bi + 2HNO₃ + 2H₂O
