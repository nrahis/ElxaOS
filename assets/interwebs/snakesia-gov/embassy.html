Visa Information & Services

🏢 Business Visa (B-SN)

For Elxa Corporation business meetings and Denali test drives

Processing time: 2-3 business days

👾 Tech Visa (T-SN)

For IT professionals and ElxaOS developers

Processing time: 24 hours (expedited for tech sector)

🏰 Tourist Visa (V-SN)

For Snake-e Mansion tours and general tourism

Processing time: 5-7 business days

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Proof of Denali appreciation (photo with any Denali)
  • ElxaOS user certificate (can be obtained at embassy)
  • Bank statement in both USD ($) and Snakes (🐍)
