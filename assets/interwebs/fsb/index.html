Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get a credit card?
A: Log into your account and click "Apply for Credit Card" in Account Actions. Cards are approved based on your credit score. Better scores unlock better cards with higher limits and lower rates!
Q: What is a credit score and how do I improve it?
A: Your credit score (300-850) reflects your financial responsibility. Make payments on time, keep credit card balances low, and avoid applying for too many cards at once. Your score updates monthly.
Q: What is a Trust Account?
A: A Trust Account is a special protected account that requires banker authorization for transactions. Perfect for family savings and rewards!
Q: How do I convert between USD and Snakes?
A: Use our built-in currency converter! The current rate is 1 USD = 2 Snakes.
Q: Is my money safe?
A: Yes! All deposits are FDICS insured and we use the latest security technology.
Q: Can I access my account from any computer?
A: Yes! Our online banking works from any ElxaNet-connected computer in Snakesia.
