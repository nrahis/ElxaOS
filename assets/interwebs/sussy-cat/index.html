About the Game

Meet Pushing Cat, the most mischievous little black cat in Snakesia! When his family leaves the house, it's time for some serious sus adventures.

Help Pushing Cat collect items from around the house while avoiding detection. Use stealth, hide in the secret Sussy Lair, and master the art of being perfectly sus!

NEW in Version 1.2:
Advanced Plotting Mechanic! Discover mysterious plot points throughout the house for special abilities and bonus points!

Game Features
  • 6 challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • 4 rooms to explore: Living Room, Kitchen, Bedroom, and Bathroom
  • Hide in the secret Sussy Lair to reduce detection
  • New Plotting Mechanic with special power-ups
  • Over 24 different items to collect
  • Retro pixel art style with smooth controls
  • Family-friendly stealth gameplay
  • Save your progress and try to beat your best times
