About the Aquarium
Where the Ocean Comes to Life

The Snake Valley Aquarium has been Snakesia's window to the underwater world for over 60 years. Founded in 1962 by marine biologist Dr. Tidal Adderworth, the aquarium houses more than 8,000 aquatic animals across 200 species — from the vibrant coral reefs of the Snakesian Coast to the mysterious creatures of the deep ocean.

Our world-class facility features 2.4 million litres of saltwater habitats, interactive touch pools, daily feeding demonstrations, and cutting-edge marine research programs. Whether you're watching sharks glide overhead or gently touching a starfish for the first time, every visit is an unforgettable ocean adventure.

Our Exhibits
Six Worlds Beneath the Waves
Shark Encounter
Walk through our 40-metre underwater tunnel as blacktip reef sharks, hammerheads, and sand tigers glide just centimetres above your head. Featuring daily shark feeding shows at 2:00 PM.
Coral Reef Experience
Immerse yourself in a living coral reef teeming with clownfish, tangs, angelfish, and sea anemones. Our reef tank recreates the vibrant ecosystems of the Snakesian Barrier Reef.
Jellyfish Gallery
A mesmerizing exhibition of bioluminescent beauty. Watch moon jellyfish, lion's mane jellyfish, and the rare Snakesian blue bell drift through colour-shifting LED displays.
Penguin Cove
Home to our colony of 24 Snakesian rockhopper penguins! Watch them waddle, swim, and play in their climate-controlled habitat. Daily feeding and enrichment sessions at 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM.
Tide Pool Touch Tank
Get hands-on with the ocean! Gently touch sea stars, hermit crabs, sea urchins, and horseshoe crabs in our supervised touch pools. A favourite with families and young marine biologists.
Marine Discovery Center
Explore the science of the sea! Examine preserved specimens, learn about ocean conservation, and discover how marine biologists study the creatures of the deep. Interactive stations for all ages.
